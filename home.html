Reforming Farming in Nepal

What keeps Nepal from truly being the "Agricultural Country" we all have been chanting about? Our team aims to give that title justice via our various tools and resources aimed for farmers and to-be farmers. The said resources will help everyone involved make informed decision with nothing but farmers' benefit in mind.

What We Do

We aim to provide comprehensive solutions that address the root challenges faced by farmers, businesses, and brands in the current agricultural ecosystem. Our key idea focus on empowering farmers, supporting agribusiness, and building sustainable business.

Help Farmers

Our initiative is committed to empowering farmers with the knowledge and resources they need to prosper. We provide a comprehensive support system that includes personalized recommendations on crop selection, optimal planting times, cultivation techniques, and strategic marketing insights. By fostering informed decision-making, we aim to enhance the livelihoods of farmers and contribute to the overall sustainability of Nepal's agricultural sector

Our Tools

We are developing a sophisticated model that recommends and consults on various aspects, such as what to grow, when to grow, how to grow, and where to sell. We provide educational resources and outreach programs for sustainable agricultural practices. As we continue to refine and expand our tools, our goal is to empower the agricultural community with the resources needed for sustainable growth and lasting impact. Till our final model is ready, we have blogs, videos, articles, etc for similar purposes.
